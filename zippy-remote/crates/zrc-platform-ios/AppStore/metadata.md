### Full Description (4000 characters max)

**ZippyRemote - Secure Remote Desktop Control**

Take control of your remote devices from your iPhone or iPad with ZippyRemote, a privacy-first remote desktop solution built with security and ease of use in mind.

**Key Features:**

• **Secure Remote Access**: Connect to your devices with end-to-end encryption and identity pinning for maximum security.

• **Intuitive Touch Controls**: Natural touch gestures for mouse control, tap for left-click, long-press for right-click, and two-finger scrolling.

• **High-Performance Rendering**: GPU-accelerated Metal rendering for smooth 60fps viewing, with support for ProMotion displays.

• **Keyboard Support**: Full keyboard input with special keys toolbar, hardware keyboard support for iPad, and Ctrl+Alt+Del support.

• **Device Management**: Easy pairing via QR code scanning, clipboard paste, or file import. Manage your device list with online/offline status.

• **Clipboard Sync**: Synchronize clipboard between your iOS device and remote desktop for seamless workflow.

• **Screen Sharing** (Limited): Share your iOS screen via ReplayKit broadcast extension (view-only, no remote input injection due to iOS limitations).

• **Privacy-First**: Your keys are stored securely in iOS Keychain with no iCloud sync. All connections use end-to-end encryption.

• **Network Resilience**: Automatic reconnection on network changes, graceful handling of WiFi to cellular transitions.

• **Native iOS Experience**: Follows Human Interface Guidelines, supports Dark Mode, Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, and all device sizes.

**Use Cases:**

• Remote IT support and troubleshooting
• Access your work computer from anywhere
• Manage home servers and devices
• Educational and training sessions
• Personal device management

**Security:**

ZippyRemote uses industry-standard encryption and follows security best practices:
- End-to-end encryption for all sessions
- Identity pinning and verification
- Secure key storage in iOS Keychain
- No data collection or tracking
- Open-source architecture

**Requirements:**

• iOS 15.0 or later
• iPhone or iPad
• Network connection (WiFi or cellular)

**Privacy:**

ZippyRemote is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect user data, track usage, or sync keys to iCloud. All connections are peer-to-peer when possible, with optional self-hosted relay servers.

**Support:**

For support, documentation, and source code, visit our project repository.

---

*ZippyRemote is part of the Zippy Remote Control (ZRC) open-source project.*
